ELECTROHUNT


Electrohunt derives its name from Electronics and Treasure-hunt. Its similar to the traditional Treasure-hunt with the only catch being that the every clue is related to technology and logic which is integral to an engineer. Hence there are no prerequisites of sorts.
The Rules are as follows:

1. Teams should consist of a minimum of 3 members and maximum of 5.
2. Selection for the Finals will be made on the basis of a Prelims, which will be a written round. (The number of teams qualifying will be decided based on the participation in the Prelims)
3. The Finals will consist of 4 levels, and needless to say, the first team to hunt down all the clues, Wins!
