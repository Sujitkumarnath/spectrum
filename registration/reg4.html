ENIAC


Spectrum'19 under the aegis of ECS, NIT Silchar presents ENIAC, a competitive programming contest designed especially for the coding geeks of first year. So get yourself ready for an engrossing experience that will test your minds to the limits. The contest shall contain 5 questions of varying difficulty, will last for 2 hours and the hosting platform will be hackerearth. The link for the contest shall be provided on the day of the contest itself.
